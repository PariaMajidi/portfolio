PARIA MAJIDI

Hi, my name is Paria.
  I am an international UX designer and researcher based in Montreal with a computer science and engineering background.
For the last 8 years, I have been working in the field of UX, in both academic and industrial settings. Currently, I work as a senior UX Designer at Canada Life. Before this role, I was a master's student at École de Technologie Supérieure in Montréal, Canada, where I focused on the User Experience of various haptic devices.
I am passionate about inclusivity and dedicated to helping people with accessibility needs, as well as the elderly and children, confidently navigate the digital world.
